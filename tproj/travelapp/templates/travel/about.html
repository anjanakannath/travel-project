About My Travel

We create simple, beautiful trip ideas made for couples and friends — quiet escapes, weekend adventures, and memory-filled getaways.

Our Story

We started as two friends who loved small trips — sunrises on beaches, late-night city walks, and short treks where we could breathe. This project grew from that love: a friendly guide to help you find beautiful experiences.

Our Mission

To make travel planning simple and joyful. We focus on moments that help people connect — scenic views, good food, and cozy stays perfect for bonding.

What You’ll Find

  • Handpicked places for couples & friend groups
  • Clear tips, ideas and mini itineraries
  • Photos to inspire your next trip

Frequently Asked

Do you book trips for us?

No — we simply share ideas & helpful information. You can book your stays or travel externally.

Is this project suitable for solo travellers?

Yes! Many places we feature are safe and peaceful for solo exploration.

Can we suggest new places?

Of course! Use the contact form to share your recommendations. We love adding new experiences.
